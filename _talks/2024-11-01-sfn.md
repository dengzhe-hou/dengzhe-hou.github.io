Poster presentation at the Society for Neuroscience (SfN) 2024 Annual Meeting. We used steady-state visual evoked potentials (SSVEPs) as a continuous neural measure to track how attention allocation guides reward-based decision-making, revealing the dynamic interplay between attentional selection and value-based choice.

**Authors:** Dengzhe Hou, Sai Sun, Satoshi Shioiri

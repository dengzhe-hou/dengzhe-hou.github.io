Oral talk at the Japanese Neuroscience Society (JNS) 2025 Annual Meeting. This presentation proposes a unified neural model for how the brain represents and modulates decision parameters in the context of emotional ambiguity, drawing on EEG and computational modeling approaches developed during self-initiated attention research.
